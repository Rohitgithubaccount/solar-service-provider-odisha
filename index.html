Solar energy is a renewable energy source that harnesses the power of the sun to generate electricity or heat. It is one of the most environmentally friendly and sustainable energy options because it reduces dependence on fossil fuels and produces no greenhouse gas emissions during operation.

Solar energy is expected to play a crucial role in the transition to clean energy. Advances in solar technology, such as more efficient panels and better energy storage solutions, continue to make solar energy more accessible and efficient. Countries are increasingly adopting policies and incentives to promote solar energy use, including tax credits, subsidies, and feed-in tariffs, which encourage both individuals and businesses to invest in solar power.
